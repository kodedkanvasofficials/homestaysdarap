about darap

The word Darap has its origin from the Limboo (Tribal) word " Tanlop" which literally means a plain or even land, which is situated at an altitude of 5,790ft. The village, which is highly dominated by the Limboo (tribal) community and other communities like Gurung, Tamang, Bhutia, Pradhan and Lepcha live together with good harmony. It occupies a place of pride in one of the richest Limboo culture heritage of Sikkim and it is a land of exquisite beauty and place of natural sightseeing especially for the zealous wanderlusts. Darap is situated at mid-way between Pelling and Yuksom- Khechuperi Road.

Why Darap?
