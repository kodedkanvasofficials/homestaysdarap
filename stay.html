About us

This Homestay is operated by a charming Limboo family at ‘Sidibung’ a part of Darap village. The family consists of mother, father of the house their two son Aita Maan and Maita Maan, daughter Suruchi and grand children.Maita Maan the elder son works away from home in a place called Yuksam so it’s Aita Maan the younger son who mostly looks after the guests. The mother and the father are most of the time engaged in the Household activities and cultivation of vegetables and crops mainly Organic.
The house is surrounded with different wild plants, flowers and orchids. The surrounding of the house is also rich in medicinal plants which the Father of the house can identify and show it to the visitors. There is a bit of climbing from the road to reach the main House. The food tradition in the house is very rich and very local. Guests are welcomed by special herbal tea of the house ‘the makee lekhama’ or the traditional maize tea. The fresh plucked vegetables from the garden beside is cooked and served in the kitchen. The food is served by the family member themselves and will be sharing the ‘Chang Den’ traditional dinner table in a Limbooo House with the guests.

The Stay

  • They are presently operating two rooms which they tend to increase in coming days.
  • The House is located on a distance of 5minutes climb from the road.
  • Non-veg is served and the family members too use meant in their food habit.
  • Pets are allowed in the House premises as the House owns Goats and Swans as theirs.
  • Smoking in the House premises is not allowed.
  • Guests are allowed in the Kitchen.
  • Tongba the fermented millet (contains Alcohol) is served.

He was connected to Tourism at a very early age because of the environment where he grew up. Today he is a very well known transporter/travel agent of Pelling where he owns Yatra Travels. His intrest today in Village Tourism and Homestays comes from his experience of life time in the field of tourism and the rise of homestay in Darap Village where he belongs. About us

Experience staying deep inside a ‘Limbooo tribe’ village with a Limbooo family and take away awe-inspiring moments along. Susen’s House is situated in the middle of the Limbooo Village of Darap just a minute or two walk from the road where the vehicle stops.
