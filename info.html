Activities

  • Butterfly & Birding : 4 – 6 days tours of Bird Photography and watching can be done making Darap Village as the base point and similarly Butterfly Photography and watching can also be done

  • Village-cum-Nature walk : Nature walks and village walks are the most interesting activity here in Darap meeting people and watching their ways of life.

  • Trekking/Hiking : Darap also forms a very perfect base for short hikes aswell as high altitude round treks to Dafey Bheer, Dzongri, Gochela and Yuksam.

  • Picnic at River belt./ River walk : Rimbi River is a nearby picnic spot where visitors can have a wonderful time.

  • Overnight Camping. / Bon-fire : Guests can also enjoy camping around the village and bonfire at the homestay itself.

  • Culture programme : traditional dance of various tribes including the famous ‘chyapbrung’ dance of Limbooo tribes can be enjoyed.

  • Foot ball Match with local talent : a football match with the young local boys would be a refreshing one in the football field of the village.

  • Sightseeing by Car : various sightseeing points can also be visited by jeep, it will be a full day or half day sightseeing.

  • Nature Biking – the visitors can enjoy both off and on road Nature Biking either down to the riverside or towards the forests of Nambu.
